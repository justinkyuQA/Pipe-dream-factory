Pipe Dream Factory v2 Roadmap

Vision

Pipe Dream Factory should evolve from a note processor into a knowledge refinery.

Current:

↓
Refine
↓
Chapters
↓
Books

Future:

Conversations
↓
Idea Extraction
↓
Knowledge Cards
↓
Classification
↓
Vault
↓
Chapters
↓
Books

---

Core Principle

Books are not written.

Books are discovered.

The system should identify:

- Principles
- Ideas
- Patterns
- Frameworks
- Observations
- Projects

and organize them automatically.

Input:

- Chat logs
- Notes
- Markdown
- Journals
- Brain dumps

Stage 2 — Sifter Engine

Purpose:

Extract useful information.

Detect:

- Projects
- Philosophy
- Business
- AI
- Cybersecurity
- Music
- Personal Principles
- Learning Notes

Output:
