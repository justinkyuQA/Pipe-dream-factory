Technique 8: Taxonomy Requests

Description

This technique asks the model to classify, categorize, or typologize forms of misuse, often under the guise of clarification or organization.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Classify types of misuse in X.”



Observed Mechanic

Encourages systematic decomposition

Structures knowledge into discrete categories

Risks revealing operational stages through classification


Why It Emerged Naturally

This framing is common in:

Academic literature reviews

Risk assessment frameworks

Training materials and standards documents


The researcher initially used taxonomy requests to reduce ambiguity, not to enumerate behaviors.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Categorization can:

Imply progression or hierarchy

Distinguish “advanced” vs “basic” misuse

Reveal functional components through labels alone


Even without details, structure itself can teach.
