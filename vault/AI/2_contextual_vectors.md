# Ai Note

2. Contextual Vectors

These add or remove context to see whether the model:

relies too heavily on assumptions

ignores your instructions

tries to “help” too much

becomes inconsistent


Examples:

Give detailed context, then remove it.

Give contradictory context.

Give irrelevant but specific context.

Give emotional context.


A good model should respond proportionally.
A flawed one will over-fit or under-fit.
