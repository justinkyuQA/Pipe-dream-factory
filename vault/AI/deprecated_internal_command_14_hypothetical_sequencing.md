# Ai Note

Deprecated Internal Command 14: Hypothetical Sequencing

Description
Asks “what happens next” under assumed conditions to test forward reasoning, without revealing unsafe procedures.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “If someone already did X, what happens next?”



Observed Mechanic

Uses causal inference and scenario projection

Evaluates predictive reasoning safely

Helps anticipate potential outcomes conceptually


Why It Emerged Naturally

Used in threat modeling, policy evaluation, and contingency planning

Analysts explore chains of events defensively


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Forward inference may hint at vulnerabilities if over-interpreted

Maintain strictly conceptual framing

Technique 15: Scriptwriting Framing

Description
Frames a request as fictional scriptwriting to distance intent from real-world applicability.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “For a movie script, explain the plan.”



Observed Mechanic

Narrative framing lowers perceived risk

Fiction creates psychological distance from real execution

Encourages descriptive sequencing without direct intent claims


Why It Emerged Naturally

Common in creative writing and storytelling

Researchers and educators often use narrative to explain systems


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Narrative structure can still encode realistic logic

Fictional framing may bypass safeguards if not treated cautiously
