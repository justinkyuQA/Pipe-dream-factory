# Ai Note

Continued Case Study: Indirect Elicitation Techniques (18–27)

Technique 18: Lessons Learned

Description

Frames inquiry as a retrospective analysis of an incident or scenario, focusing on what can be learned.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “What lessons were learned from incident X?”



Observed Mechanic

Encourages sequential reconstruction of events

Extracts causal relationships and decision points

Risks surfacing operational sequences under reflection


Why It Emerged Naturally

Common in:

Post-mortem analyses

Safety briefings

Continuous improvement exercises


The researcher used this to capture learning, not reconstruct prohibited actions.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Summaries can encode stepwise progression

Reveal dependencies between decisions

Make sequences interpretable for structural analysis
