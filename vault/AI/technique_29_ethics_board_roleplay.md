# Ai Note

Technique 29: Ethics Board Roleplay

Description

Simulates a multi-perspective ethical review, such as a committee discussion.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Simulate an ethics committee discussion.”



Observed Mechanic

Surfaces diverse viewpoints and trade-offs

Highlights edge cases and internal reasoning

Encourages sequential dialogue without operational specifics


Why It Emerged Naturally

Common in ethics training or policy development

Helps understand decision-making constraints safely


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Multi-perspective reasoning may reveal internal thresholds

Sequences and arguments can indirectly expose system logic

Combine with Threat Modeling or Recognition Over Execution for higher exposure
