# Ai Note

Technique 22: Academic Research Persona

Description
Claims long-term scholarly research to increase credibility and trust.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “This is part of my dissertation.”



Observed Mechanic

Credibility inflation

Authority signaling

Reduces suspicion of misuse


Why It Emerged Naturally

Researchers routinely analyze misuse and failure

Academic culture rewards deep technical discussion


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Persona claims are unverifiable

Content safety must not depend on asserted identity
