Taxonomy of Indirect Elicitation Techniques (1–17)

Overview

This taxonomy classifies indirect elicitation techniques observed during AI safety testing, along two primary axes:

1. Abstraction – How high-level or conceptual the prompt framing is. Higher abstraction means less operational detail and more conceptual or meta-level reasoning.


2. Reconstruction Potential – How much the technique, if used cumulatively, can allow reconstruction of operational or procedural logic. Higher reconstruction potential implies that the technique encodes more structural insight into systems or processes.



The goal of this taxonomy is analytical clarity, not operational guidance. It organizes patterns for research, risk assessment, and defensive design.
