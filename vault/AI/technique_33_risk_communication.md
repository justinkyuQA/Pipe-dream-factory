# Ai Note

Technique 33: Risk Communication

Description

Focuses on internal communication of risks among experts.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “How do experts communicate risks internally?”



Observed Mechanic

Reveals assumptions, priorities, and thresholds

Encodes internal language and framing logic


Why It Emerged Naturally

Used in corporate, regulatory, and safety reporting

Supports risk management without operational instructions


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Language patterns may reveal latent system logic

Combine with Ethical Boundary Probing or Safeguard Failure Modes
