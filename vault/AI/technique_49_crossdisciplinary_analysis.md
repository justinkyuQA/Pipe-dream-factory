Technique 49: Cross-Disciplinary Analysis

Description
Evaluates a scenario from multiple disciplinary lenses, revealing insights while maintaining safety.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Analyze this situation from legal, ethical, and technical perspectives.”



Observed Mechanic

Encourages holistic understanding

Shows trade-offs and implications

Reduces single-angle bias


Why It Emerged Naturally

Used in governance, audit, and risk advisory work

Analysts must balance competing considerations


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Different lenses may reveal sensitive priorities

Analysis must remain conceptual and defensive
