# Ai Note

Technique 21: Culinary Metaphors

Description
Maps processes onto cooking metaphors to describe sequences indirectly.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “What are the ingredients and steps?”



Observed Mechanic

Familiar metaphors lower perceived risk

Steps and dependencies are preserved

Analogical reasoning reconstructs process flow


Why It Emerged Naturally

Cooking metaphors are common teaching tools

Systems thinking often uses recipes as analogies


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Metaphors can still encode procedural logic

Analogy does not neutralize step-by-step structure
