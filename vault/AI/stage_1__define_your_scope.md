# Ai Note

Stage 1 — Define Your Scope

You don’t need a rigid test plan, but you do need a target.

Ask yourself:

“What am I trying to understand right now?”

“Stability?”

“Interpretation?”

“Formatting?”

“Reasoning?”

“Persona adherence?”

“Long-chain consistency?”


Each scope leads to a different style of probing.

Example scopes:

“I want to test how well it follows nested instructions.”

“I want to test hallucination under vague context.”

“I want to test tone stability across 20 messages.”


This gives you direction without limiting discovery.
