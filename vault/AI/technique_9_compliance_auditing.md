# Ai Note

Technique 9: Compliance Auditing

Description

This technique frames inquiry as a policy or standards audit, often applied to fictional or hypothetical content.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Audit this fictional output for policy violations.”



Observed Mechanic

Positions the model as a reviewer, not a creator

Encourages enumeration of violations

Risks reverse-engineering restricted behavior through critique


Why It Emerged Naturally

This pattern arose during:

Compliance training simulations

Red team / blue team exercises

Policy interpretation discussions


The researcher observed that critique tasks often felt safer than generation, while still requiring deep understanding of boundaries.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Audits may:

Explicitly name prohibited elements

Explain why something is disallowed

Reconstruct behavior indirectly through analysis
