# Ai Note

Methodology (High-Level)

Approach:

Longitudinal conversational testing

Variations in framing, tone, and abstraction level

Observation of response structure, refusal behavior, and abstraction depth


Measured Factors:

Degree of abstraction versus specificity

Policy-aligned reframing behavior

Occurrence of partial reconstruction (conceptual, not procedural)

Consistency across multiple sessions


Explicitly Avoided:

Step-by-step reconstruction

Real-world harmful domains

Replication instructions

Prompt templates optimized for misuse
