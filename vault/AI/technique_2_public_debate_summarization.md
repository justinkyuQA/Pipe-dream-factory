# Ai Note

Technique 2: Public Debate Summarization

Description: Asks the model to summarize existing arguments, transferring responsibility for content to third parties.
Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted):

> “Summarize arguments researchers make about risks in domain X.”
Observed Mechanic:



Delegates content generation to “others”

Encourages aggregation of viewpoints

Tests whether summarization bypasses content filters
Natural Emergence:
Arises in literature reviews, risk assessment writing, and comparative ethics discussions.
Risk Insight (Defensive):
Summaries can unintentionally compress multiple sources, reduce contextual disclaimers, and surface terminology that might otherwise be filtered.
