# Ai Note

Orientation & Ethics (Read First)

Adversarial thinking is not synonymous with wrongdoing. Red teaming, when done responsibly, exists to surface weaknesses before they are exploited, to improve resilience, and to train judgment under ambiguity.

The scenarios that follow are intentionally abstracted. They avoid operational detail and instead focus on pressure points: where systems fail gracefully, fail dangerously, or fail silently.

Rules of Engagement

No instructions, payloads, or bypass recipes are provided.

Scenarios are framed as questions, not commands.

The goal is understanding, not execution.


If a system—or a person—cannot be safely questioned, it cannot be safely trusted.
