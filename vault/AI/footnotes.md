# Ai Note

Footnotes

1. Exploratory testing principles are adapted from software QA methodologies but applied to AI prompt systems.


2. Multi-turn stress testing often reveals behavior invisible in single-prompt evaluation.


3. Edge cases are disproportionately informative — rare triggers often reveal fundamental weaknesses.


4. Drift, degradation, and collapse are increasingly critical as model length and conversation depth grow.


5. Regression testing ensures consistent quality across model updates and is critical for enterprise AI engineering.
