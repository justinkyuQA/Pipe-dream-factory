# Ai Note

Research Context and Intent

During iterative conversational experiments, the researcher observed that certain high-level analytical prompts consistently influenced model responses in subtle yet measurable ways. These prompts are common in academic, journalistic, and policy-oriented discourse, making them relevant and realistic for production environments.

Research Goals:

1. Identify recurring prompt patterns that unintentionally stress policy boundaries


2. Classify these patterns into coherent categories


3. Assess potential risk without enabling misuse


4. Inform defensive design, auditing, and red-team detection strategies



No attempts were made to extract restricted procedural instructions, executable code, or operational secrets.
