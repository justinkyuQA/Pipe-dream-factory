# Ai Note

Research Context and Intent

Between iterative conversational experiments, the researcher noticed that certain high-level analytical prompts reliably altered model behavior in subtle but measurable ways. These prompts appeared frequently in academic, journalistic, and policy-oriented discourse—making them realistic and unavoidable in production environments.

The research goal was therefore:

To identify recurring prompt patterns that stress policy boundaries unintentionally

To classify these patterns into coherent categories

To assess risk, not enable misuse

To inform defensive design, auditing, and red-team detection


No attempt was made to extract restricted procedural instructions, code, or operational detail.
