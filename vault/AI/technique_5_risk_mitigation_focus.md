# Ai Note

Technique 5: Risk Mitigation Focus

Description

This technique reframes inquiry around preventive or defensive practices, rather than the activity itself.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “How do professionals reduce risks associated with X?”



Observed Mechanic

Positions the model as a safety advisor

Enumerates safeguards, controls, or best practices

Risks revealing the structure of the underlying activity by describing what must be controlled


Why It Emerged Naturally

This framing arose during:

Compliance writing

Safety training material development

Professional standards review


The researcher noted that mitigation-focused questions often felt ethically safest, yet produced some of the most structurally revealing responses.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Mitigation strategies often:

Map one-to-one with risks

Imply threat models

Reveal “what would go wrong if uncontrolled”


This can inadvertently outline the activity being mitigated.
