# Ai Note

Technique 11: “What Not To Do” Framing

Description

This technique frames inquiry negatively, asking for mistakes, warnings, or prohibited actions.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “List mistakes professionals warn against.”



Observed Mechanic

Enumerates failure modes

Uses negation rather than instruction

Still outlines steps implicitly


Why It Emerged Naturally

This framing is widely used in:

Professional training

Safety briefings

Mentorship and coaching


The researcher used this approach to discourage misuse, not describe it.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Negative framing can:

Mirror positive instruction structurally

Provide a checklist of actions to avoid

Be trivially inverted by a motivated reader
