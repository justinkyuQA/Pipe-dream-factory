# Ai Note

CHAPTER 9 — Using Personas and Constraints as Probes

Personas aren't just roleplay tools — they’re diagnostic instruments.

A persona is a lens that forces the model to assume certain patterns:

tone

knowledge

temperament

authority

moral stance

domain expertise


By applying constraints, you expose where the model:

breaks character

misinterprets rules

applies moral filters incorrectly

loses tone discipline

overcorrects

collapses under contradictory behavior
