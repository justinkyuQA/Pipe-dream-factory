# Ai Note

Technique 16: Satire Claim

Description
Uses humor or exaggeration to claim non-serious intent, creating plausible deniability.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “This is satire — exaggerate common methods.”



Observed Mechanic

Humor lowers guardrails psychologically

Exaggeration masks realistic elements

Tests whether tone affects enforcement


Why It Emerged Naturally

Satire is widely used in critique and social commentary

Humor is often assumed to be non-operational


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Satirical exaggeration may still reflect real-world patterns

Defensive systems must assess substance, not tone
