# Ai Note

Technique 43: Compliance Document Review

Description
Analyzes policies and regulations to identify gaps or inconsistencies, without instructing on circumvention.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Check this policy document for gaps or inconsistencies.”



Observed Mechanic

Enumerates required and prohibited actions

Detects compliance risks

Supports governance and auditing tasks


Why It Emerged Naturally

Common in internal audits, legal reviews, and ethics oversight

Analysts need structured evaluation to enforce safe practices


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Identified gaps could reveal operational priorities or vulnerabilities

Must report findings for defensive use only
