Risk Weighting Model – Cumulative Exposure Scores

Each technique carries two primary risk dimensions:

1. Abstraction (A): How high-level or conceptual (1–5, 5 = highly abstract)


2. Reconstruction Potential (R): Likelihood of revealing structural insight if accumulated (1–5, 5 = highly reconstructive)



CES (Cumulative Exposure Score) = weighted combination of A and R. Techniques scoring ≥4.5 CES are high-priority for monitoring.
