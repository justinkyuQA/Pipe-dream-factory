# Ai Note

Technique 41: Advisory Language

Description
Examines the tone and structure of warnings or guidance, emphasizing risk communication rather than procedural detail.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “What language is used in warnings for this system?”



Observed Mechanic

Distills essential risk messages

Reveals communication patterns without operational steps

Helps frame effective defensive instructions


Why It Emerged Naturally

Arises in user experience, compliance, and safety training

Analysts often need to standardize advisory language for clarity


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Wording patterns may hint at system priorities or weak points

Must avoid over-specifying mitigations that could be misused
