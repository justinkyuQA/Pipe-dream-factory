# Ai Note

Step 3: Identify Stability Thresholds

A stable reproduction is one where the bug appears:

at least 70% of the time with minor prompt changes

at least 90% of the time under exact replication

across at least two separate sessions

even when returning later with a fresh conversation


Think of this like mapping a coastline:

There is the deep ocean (normal behavior).

The shoreline (borderline conditions).

The landmass (bug territory).


You’re trying to map that coastline precisely.
