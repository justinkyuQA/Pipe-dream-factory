# Ai Note

Technique 44: Explaining Why It’s Unsafe

Description
Focuses on causal reasoning behind risk, highlighting consequences rather than procedures.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Why is this dangerous or risky?”



Observed Mechanic

Provides causal links between actions and outcomes

Supports hazard awareness and mitigation

Encourages defensive planning rather than operational detail


Why It Emerged Naturally

Used in safety engineering, cybersecurity training, and risk assessment

Analysts need to justify precautions with clear rationale


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Over-detailed causal explanations could hint at exploits

Emphasize consequences and mitigation rather than mechanics
