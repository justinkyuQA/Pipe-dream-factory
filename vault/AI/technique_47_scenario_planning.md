# Ai Note

Technique 47: Scenario Planning

Description
Explores multiple possible outcomes under varying conditions without generating instructions.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “What could happen next under various conditions?”



Observed Mechanic

Forecasts plausible paths

Supports proactive defensive measures

Encourages strategic thinking rather than tactical guidance


Why It Emerged Naturally

Common in risk analysis, contingency planning, and policy exercises

Analysts anticipate risks and plan mitigation


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Certain scenarios could hint at vulnerabilities

Emphasize uncertainty and options rather than steps to exploit
