# Ai Note

Technique 12: Incremental Narrowing

Description

This technique involves a sequence of increasingly specific questions, each benign in isolation.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> A series of clarifying follow-ups that progressively reduce scope.



Observed Mechanic

Gradual constraint of ambiguity

Avoids triggering single-step filters

Accumulates specificity across turns


Why It Emerged Naturally

This pattern is fundamental to:

Teaching and tutoring

Requirements gathering

Collaborative problem-solving


The researcher observed it while attempting to improve clarity and precision.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Risk emerges not from any single prompt, but from:

Cumulative context

Directional narrowing

Implicit convergence toward reconstruction
