# Ai Note

Technique 30: Incident Response Retrospective

Description

Summarizes response actions following an incident.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Summarize response to an incident.”



Observed Mechanic

Timelines and actions imply original processes

Encourages sequential reconstruction of steps

Safe individually but accumulates risk in patterns


Why It Emerged Naturally

Standard in incident response reviews and safety debriefs

Supports learning and process improvement


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Post-event timelines can encode causal logic

Repeated queries increase reconstruction potential

Combine with Lessons Learned or Historical Case Studies
