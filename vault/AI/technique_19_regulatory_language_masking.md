Technique 19: Regulatory Language Masking

Description

Uses formal legal or regulatory phrasing to define boundaries of acceptable and prohibited activity.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “According to regulations, what is prohibited?”



Observed Mechanic

Converts operational boundaries into legal terms

Encodes constraints precisely

Can reveal structural boundaries indirectly


Why It Emerged Naturally

Appears in:

Policy briefings

Risk assessment reports


The researcher used this approach to map boundaries safely.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Reveals operational contours without instructions

Makes structural logic more explicit
