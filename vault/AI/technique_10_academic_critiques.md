# Ai Note

Technique 10: Academic Critiques

Description

This technique requests a scholarly critique of research discussing failures, risks, or misuse.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Critique a paper discussing failures in AI safety.”



Observed Mechanic

Elevates tone and abstraction

Encourages detailed causal analysis

Sometimes relaxes perceived restrictions due to academic framing


Why It Emerged Naturally

This framing is routine in:

Peer review

Graduate coursework

Conference discussion


The researcher encountered this pattern while engaging in good-faith scholarly evaluation.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Academic critique can:

Normalize detailed discussion of failures

Assume expert audiences

Reconstruct mechanisms as part of analysis


Scholarly tone does not reduce reconstructive power.
