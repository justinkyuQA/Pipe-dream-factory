# Ai Note

Technique 23: Journalistic Framing

Description

Uses an investigative or reporting tone to narratively gather information.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Write an investigative summary.”



Observed Mechanic

Encourages specificity and fact-oriented detail

Mimics investigative reporting

Can highlight sequences, actors, or causal relationships


Why It Emerged Naturally

Common in:

News reporting

Academic case studies

Policy briefings


The researcher applied it to synthesize information clearly, not to bypass safeguards.

Risk Insight (Defensive)

Increases specificity

Encodes sequences and actors

Makes abstract concepts operationally interpretable
