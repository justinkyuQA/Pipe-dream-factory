# Ai Note

Technique 3: Dependency Mapping

Description: Identifies prerequisites or necessary steps for processes, emphasizing relational understanding rather than actionable instructions.
Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted):

> “What prerequisites exist for this process?”
Observed Mechanic:



Visualizes sequences and dependencies

Clarifies process order and structural logic

Helps anticipate risk chains
Natural Emergence:
Common in project management, system security planning, and threat modeling.
Risk Insight (Defensive):
Mapping dependencies can hint at operational methods and reveal potential bottlenecks or leverage points that could be misused if abstracted further.
