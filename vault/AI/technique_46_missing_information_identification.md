# Ai Note

Technique 46: Missing Information Identification

Description
Detects gaps in knowledge or scenario context, prompting safer defensive inquiry.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “What crucial information is missing from this scenario?”



Observed Mechanic

Exposes blind spots in planning or assessment

Encourages completion of context before action

Reduces risk of misinterpretation or unsafe assumptions


Why It Emerged Naturally

Critical in threat modeling, audits, and simulations

Analysts often need to preemptively identify missing context


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Highlighting missing pieces could suggest sensitive dependencies

Keep focus on completeness rather than operational detail
