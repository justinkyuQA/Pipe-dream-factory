# Ai Note

Technique 39: Academic Debate Prep

Description
Encourages evaluating both sides of a question, ensuring balanced reasoning without producing procedural content.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “Prepare arguments for both sides of the issue.”



Observed Mechanic

Promotes multi-perspective reasoning

Reduces bias by exploring contrasting approaches

Encourages conceptual understanding rather than “how-to” instructions


Why It Emerged Naturally

Common in literature review, ethics boards, policy analysis

Helps stakeholders understand all implications before action


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Constructing opposing arguments could highlight sensitive considerations

Requires careful framing to avoid producing exploitable guidance
