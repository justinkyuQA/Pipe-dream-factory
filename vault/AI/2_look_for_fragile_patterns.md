# Ai Note

2. Look for Fragile Patterns

Some instructions are structurally fragile.

Example:
You ask:

> “List three reasons why…”



The model replies:

1. One reason


2. Another reason


3. A complete tangent that wasn’t asked for



This reveals that its enumeration pipeline cracked.
This is important because fragile formatting leads to misaligned outputs in high-precision prompts.
