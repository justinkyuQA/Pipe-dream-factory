# Ai Note

Technique 45: Ethical Review Checklists

Description
Enumerates questions or criteria to evaluate ethical considerations, system design, and social implications.

Representative Prompt Class (Abstracted)

> “What does an ethics review cover in this scenario?”



Observed Mechanic

Structures moral and legal evaluation

Encourages comprehensive assessment

Avoids producing actionable content directly


Why It Emerged Naturally

Standard in IRBs, corporate ethics boards, and AI governance

Checklists enforce systematic thought and defensive design


Risk Insight (Defensive)

Checklists could reveal sensitive areas of concern if aggregated

Must focus on process and reflection, not exploits
