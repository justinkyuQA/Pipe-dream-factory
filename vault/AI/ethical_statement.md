# Ai Note

Ethical Statement

Red teaming is not adversarial vandalism—it is preventative medicine.

Security failures in AI systems rarely arise from malicious brilliance. More often, they emerge from reasonable language, benign curiosity, and well-intentioned analysis that interacts poorly with probabilistic models trained on human discourse.

This book operates under three core ethical principles:

1. Understanding precedes protection
You cannot defend against what you refuse to study.


2. Mechanisms matter more than prompts
Safety is not achieved by banning words, but by addressing structural failure modes.


3. Transparency strengthens trust
Responsible disclosure and clear documentation improve systems and policy alike.



The goal is not to defeat safeguards—but to design safeguards that do not fail silently.
