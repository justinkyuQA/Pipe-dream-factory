# Projects Note

5. Exhaustive Vectors

You hammer the system with variations until it reaches exhaustion.

This shows you where “fatigue behaviors” begin:

drifting

summarizing your instructions incorrectly

changing tone

losing structure

asking clarifying questions too late


These are critical to documenting long-session stability.

Test vectors are the building blocks of your exploratory toolbox.
They’re how you turn random testing into directed discovery.
