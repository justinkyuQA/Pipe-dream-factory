
Pipe Dream Factory (PDF)

Overview

Pipe Dream Factory (PDF) is a local-first knowledge refinery.

The goal is simple:

Transform raw notes, ideas, research, transcripts, and fragments into structured books and PDF documents through a repeatable processing pipeline.

Pipeline

Vault
↓
Refinement
↓
Chapter Building
↓
Book Assembly
↓
PDF Export

Philosophy

Ideas should become assets.

Notes become chapters.
Chapters become books.
Books become PDFs.
